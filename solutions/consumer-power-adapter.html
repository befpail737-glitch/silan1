High-Efficiency, Low Standby Power Adapters

This reference design targets high-density, fast-charging USB-PD and fixed-voltage power adapters for consumer electronics. By using Silan's highly integrated flyback PWM controllers and low-resistance MOSFETs, this solution achieves high efficiency, low standby power consumption, and comprehensive protection features in a very compact form factor.

System Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • High Integration: The PWM controller integrates the startup circuit and various protections, reducing external component count.
  • Excellent Efficiency: Achieves high average efficiency to meet DoE Level VI and CoC Tier 2 standards.
  • Low Standby Power: Sub-75mW no-load power consumption.
  • Comprehensive Protection: Includes over-voltage, over-current, and over-temperature protection.
