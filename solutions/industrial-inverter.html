Industrial Control

High-Reliability Solution for Industrial Motor Drives

This solution provides a comprehensive reference design for a three-phase industrial inverter (frequency converter) capable of driving AC induction motors. It leverages Silan's high-performance IGBTs, robust gate drivers, and powerful 32-bit MCUs to achieve precise motor control, high efficiency, and long-term reliability required in demanding industrial environments.

Core Advantages

Why choose this Silan-based solution for your industrial inverter.

High Efficiency

Utilizes Silan's latest Field-Stop Trench IGBTs to minimize conduction and switching losses, achieving efficiency greater than 95%.

Robust Protection

Integrated gate drivers provide over-current, short-circuit, and under-voltage protection for safe and reliable operation.

Precise Control

The 32-bit MCU enables advanced vector control algorithms for smooth and accurate motor performance across all speed ranges.

High Reliability

Components are designed and tested for harsh industrial conditions, ensuring long-term reliability and minimal downtime.
