High-Reliability Solution for Industrial Motor Drives

This solution provides a comprehensive reference design for a three-phase industrial inverter (frequency converter) capable of driving AC induction motors. It leverages Silan's high-performance IGBTs, robust gate drivers, and powerful 32-bit MCUs to achieve precise motor control, high efficiency, and long-term reliability required in demanding industrial environments.

System Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • High Efficiency: Utilizes Silan's latest Field-Stop Trench IGBTs to minimize conduction and switching losses.
  • Robust Protection: Integrated gate drivers provide over-current, short-circuit, and under-voltage protection.
  • Precise Control: The 32-bit MCU enables advanced vector control algorithms for smooth and accurate motor performance.
  • High Reliability: Components are designed and tested for harsh industrial conditions.
