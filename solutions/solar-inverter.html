Maximum Power Point Tracking for Photovoltaic Systems

This solution for solar photovoltaic (PV) inverters is designed for maximum efficiency and reliability. It features Silan's advanced power semiconductors, including Silicon Carbide (SiC) diodes and high-speed IGBTs, to enable higher switching frequencies and reduce power losses. The design supports advanced Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) algorithms, ensuring the maximum possible power is harvested from the solar panels under varying conditions.

System Block Diagram

Core Advantages

  • Ultra-High Efficiency: SiC components enable peak efficiencies of over 98%.
  • Wide MPPT Range: Allows for flexible solar array configurations and maximizes energy harvesting.
  • Advanced Cooling: Optimized thermal design for high power density and long life.
  • Grid Support Features: Includes functions like reactive power control and low-voltage ride-through.
