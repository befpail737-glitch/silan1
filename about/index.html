Your Trusted Partner for Silan Semiconductors

LITONG is a leading, officially authorized first-level distributor for Silan Microelectronics. With over a decade of experience in the electronics distribution industry, we have built a reputation for reliability, technical expertise, and unparalleled customer service. Our mission is to empower innovators by providing them with high-quality semiconductor components and the expert support they need to bring their ideas to life.

Our Commitment to Quality

We are an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, a reflection of our rigorous quality management system. We guarantee that all products we supply are 100% authentic and factory-traceable. Our state-of-the-art warehouse and handling processes ensure that components are stored and shipped with the utmost care.

Expert FAE Team

Our greatest asset is our team of experienced Field Application Engineers (FAEs). With deep knowledge of Silan's product portfolio and a wide range of applications, our FAEs work as an extension of your design team. They provide technical support, design reviews, and product recommendations to help you accelerate your development cycle and overcome technical challenges.

Authorization Certificate

We proudly display our authorization from Silan as a testament to our direct partnership. This ensures you receive genuine products, competitive pricing, and direct factory support.
