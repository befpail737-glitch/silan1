TechAuto Inc. - Reducing Production Lead Times by 85%

Customer

TechAuto Inc.

California, USA

Industry

Automotive Manufacturing

EV Powertrain Systems

Challenge

Long lead times affecting production schedules

Solution

Local LA warehouse + VMI program

The Challenge

TechAuto Inc., a leading manufacturer of EV powertrain systems, was facing critical supply chain challenges. Their previous distributor required 3-4 weeks for delivery, causing production delays and forcing them to maintain excessive inventory levels.

  • 21-day average lead time from international distributor
  • $2.5M tied up in safety stock
  • Production delays averaging 5 days per month
  • Emergency air freight costs exceeding $150K annually

The Solution

LITONG implemented a comprehensive supply chain solution leveraging our Los Angeles warehouse and Vendor Managed Inventory (VMI) program:

  • Local Stock: Maintained 90-day inventory of critical Silan IGBTs and MCUs at LA warehouse
  • VMI Program: Automated replenishment based on consumption patterns
  • FAE Support: Dedicated technical engineer for design optimization
  • Consignment: Pay-as-you-consume model reducing working capital

The Results

85% Lead Time Reduction

From 21 days to 3 days

$1.8M Inventory Cost Savings

Reduced safety stock requirements

28% Component Cost Reduction

vs previous distributor pricing

Zero Production Delays

In 18 months since partnership

"LITONG transformed our supply chain. The local warehouse and VMI program eliminated our stockout issues entirely. We've saved millions in inventory costs and our production line hasn't stopped once for component shortages."

John Mitchell VP of Supply Chain, TechAuto Inc.
