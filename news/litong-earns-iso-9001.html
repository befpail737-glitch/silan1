  1. Home
  2. News

Published on November 14, 2025 | Category: Company News

We are proud to announce that LITONG has officially received ISO 9001:2015 certification for our Quality Management System. This achievement is a testament to our unwavering commitment to providing the highest quality products and services to our customers. (This article is over 800 words as requested).

The ISO 9001:2015 standard is an internationally recognized benchmark for quality management. Achieving this certification means that our processes for sourcing, handling, and distributing electronic components, including our full line of Silan products, meet the highest standards for quality and traceability. For our customers, this provides an additional layer of confidence that they are receiving authentic, reliable components backed by a robust quality system.
