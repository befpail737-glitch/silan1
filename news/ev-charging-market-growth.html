EV Charging Market to Triple by 2030

  1. Home
  2. News
  3. EV Charging Growth
Published on November 10, 2025 | Category: Industry News | By Industry Analyst

A new report from a leading market research firm predicts that the global market for electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure will more than triple by 2030. This rapid expansion is driven by government mandates, increasing consumer adoption of EVs, and significant investments in public charging networks.

The EV Charging Opportunity

This growth presents a tremendous opportunity for semiconductor manufacturers and distributors. EV chargers, particularly DC fast chargers, are power-dense systems that rely on high-performance power semiconductors to function. Key components include:

IGBTs and SiC MOSFETs

For the high-power PFC and DC/DC conversion stages in fast chargers.

Gate Drivers

To efficiently switch the power transistors with minimal losses.

MCUs

For control, communication, and user interface management.

Sensors

For monitoring temperature, current, and voltage for safety.

Market Drivers

Several factors are driving this explosive growth in the EV charging market:

  • Government Mandates: Many countries have set ambitious targets for EV adoption, requiring massive investments in charging infrastructure.
  • Consumer Adoption: As EVs become more affordable and have longer ranges, more consumers are making the switch.
  • Technological Advances: Fast charging technology is improving, making EV charging more convenient.
  • Corporate Fleets: Companies are electrifying their fleets, creating demand for workplace and depot charging solutions.
