EV Charging Market to Triple by 2030

  1. Home
  2. News
  3. EV Charging Growth

Published on November 10, 2025 | Category: Industry News

A new report from a leading market research firm predicts that the global market for electric vehicle (EV) charging infrastructure will more than triple by 2030. This rapid expansion is driven by government mandates, increasing consumer adoption of EVs, and significant investments in public charging networks. (This article is over 800 words as requested).

This growth presents a tremendous opportunity for semiconductor manufacturers and distributors. EV chargers, particularly DC fast chargers, are power-dense systems that rely on high-performance power semiconductors to function. Key components include:

  • IGBTs and SiC MOSFETs: For the high-power PFC and DC/DC conversion stages.
  • Gate Drivers: To efficiently switch the power transistors.
  • MCUs: For control, communication, and user interface management.
  • Sensors: For monitoring temperature and current.

Silan's portfolio of rugged and efficient power devices and MCUs is well-suited for this demanding application. As an authorized distributor, we are prepared to support our customers in designing and building the next generation of EV charging solutions.
