Frequently Asked Questions

What is an IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit)?

An IMU is a device that combines multiple sensors, typically an accelerometer and a gyroscope, to measure and report a body's specific force, angular rate, and sometimes orientation. Silan's IMUs provide a compact solution for motion tracking.

What are some common applications for MEMS sensors?

MEMS sensors are used everywhere! Common applications include image stabilization in cameras, navigation in drones and robots, activity tracking in wearables, and safety systems in automobiles.
