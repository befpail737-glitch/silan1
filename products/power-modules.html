Integrated Solutions for Simplified Power Design

Silan's Intelligent Power Modules (IPMs) integrate power devices (IGBTs or MOSFETs) with gate drivers, protection circuits, and other components into a single, compact package. This high level of integration simplifies the design process, reduces board space, and improves the reliability and performance of motor control and power conversion systems. Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of using an IPM over discrete components?

IPMs offer several advantages, including a smaller footprint, reduced design complexity, improved thermal performance, and enhanced reliability due to integrated protection features like over-current, over-temperature, and under-voltage lockout.

How do I select the right IPM for my motor control application?

Key parameters to consider are the motor's voltage and current ratings, the required switching frequency, and the control strategy (e.g., FOC, BLDC). Our FAE team can assist you in selecting the optimal IPM for your specific needs.
